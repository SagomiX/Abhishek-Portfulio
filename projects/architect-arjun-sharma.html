About Me

Architecture Is My Language,
Your Space Is My Canvas

I am Ar. Arjun Sharma — an architect and interior designer based in Chandigarh with over a decade of experience creating spaces that balance beauty, function, and identity.

What sets my practice apart is simple: I believe every client deserves my personal attention. You won't be handed to a junior team. From our first meeting to the day you walk into your finished space, I am with you.

My work spans private residences, commercial spaces, hospitality projects, and institutional buildings — each shaped by the unique story of the people who will live or work in them.
