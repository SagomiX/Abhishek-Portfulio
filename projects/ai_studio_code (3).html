Brooklyn Roast

Located in the heart of the concrete jungle, Empire Roast isn't just a café. It's a sanctuary for creators, thinkers, and New Yorkers who demand a better cup. We source our beans directly from sustainable farms and roast them in small batches right here in the city.


Every pour tells a story of craftsmanship, from our 18-hour cold brew to our signature gold-flecked lattes.
