The Grand Trunk Road

2600 km of Flavour,
Under One Roof

Inspired by one of Asia's longest and most ancient roads — the Grand Trunk Road stretching from Kabul to Chittagong — The GT Road is a culinary pilgrimage through history. Every dish carries the soul of a city along this legendary route.

Step inside and you step into another era. Jharokha windows, weapons as showpieces, a vintage car at the entrance, dimly lit earthy tones, and the aroma of coal-fired kebabs. Rustic Indian heritage meets modern-day dining theatre.

The GT Road Experience

Grill. Feast.
Celebrate.

The GT Road reinvented the art of dining with its signature concept — a live BBQ grill set on every table. You don't just eat here; you cook, you interact, you celebrate the food as it sizzles in front of you.

From the live chaat counters to the rotating main course buffet where a new dish is added every single day — no two visits to The GT Road are ever the same.

  • BBQ grill on every table — a truly interactive dining experience
  • Mughal-era royal décor transporting you back in time
  • Afghani, Punjabi, Mughlai — all three cuisines in one sitting
  • Family-friendly with special luncheon buffets on weekdays
  • Private room available for celebrations & corporate events
