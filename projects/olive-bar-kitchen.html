Our Story

Where Heritage
Meets Gastronomy

Nestled within a restored Mughal-era haveli in Mehrauli, Olive Bar & Kitchen is Delhi's most iconic upscale dining destination. Our whitewashed walls, arched windows, and leafy courtyard shaded by an ancient banyan tree create an atmosphere unlike any other in the city.

Under the direction of Chef Dhruv Oberoi — who trained alongside legendary Spanish chef Ferran Adrià — our kitchen crafts a globally inspired Mediterranean menu that celebrates fresh ingredients, bold flavours, and artful presentation.

From fairy-lit terrace dinners with a distant view of the Qutub Minar to live music evenings and vibrant Sunday brunches, Olive offers not just a meal, but an experience that lingers long after the last course.
