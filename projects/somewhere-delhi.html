Our Story

Chic. Cosy.
Completely Somewhere.

Somewhere Delhi is where Connaught Place comes to exhale. Tucked into H-10, Laxmi Building on the Ground Floor of CP's H Block, it's the kind of place that doesn't announce itself — you discover it, and then you keep coming back.

The décor strikes the perfect balance between chic and cozy — elegant ambience meets a laid-back spirit. Whether you're here for a long Sunday brunch, an evening cocktail ritual, or a full-on birthday bash, Somewhere has a corner that feels made for exactly that moment.

"A refreshing blend of elegant ambiance, attentive service, and a well-curated menu that caters to both Indian and global palates."

From small plates and innovative cocktails to North Indian classics, Italian pasta and Oriental delights — the menu is a well-curated journey that keeps every table happy.
