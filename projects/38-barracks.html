Our Story

Designed as a
Tribute to the
Armed Forces

38 Barracks isn't just another restaurant in Connaught Place — it's a living tribute to India's brave armed forces. Inspired by the home of a decorated retired British-Indian Army colonel, every corner of 38 Barracks tells a story of honour, heritage, and hospitality.

From war medals and sepia-toned photographs on the walls to antique weapons and vintage officer-mess furnishings — the interiors transport you to a different era while the menu brings you the best of today's culinary world. North Indian. Continental. Italian. Oriental. All under one legendary roof.
