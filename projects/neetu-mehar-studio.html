About the Studio

The Craft Behind
Every Radiant Bride

Neetu Mehar Studio is one of Delhi's most sought-after bridal and beauty studios. Founded with a deep passion for the transformative power of makeup, the studio has been creating breathtaking looks for hundreds of brides across Delhi-NCR and beyond.

With advanced training from India's top industry leaders, every look from Neetu's brush is precise, soulful, and built to last through the longest of celebrations.
