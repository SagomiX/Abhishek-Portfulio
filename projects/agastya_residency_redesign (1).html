Our Story
A Sanctuary in the
Heart of the Himalayas

Nestled on the Dharamshala–McLeod Ganj road, Agastya Residency is a newly built boutique hotel offering the perfect blend of modern comfort and Himalayan tranquillity.

Our 12 thoughtfully designed rooms provide breathtaking valley and mountain views, making every morning feel like an escape. The Dhauladhar ranges greet you from your balcony, while the spiritual energy of McLeod Ganj is just minutes away.

From our in-house restaurant serving North Indian, South Indian, Gujarati, Chinese, and Continental cuisine, to our attentive staff — every detail is crafted for your comfort.

Dining
A Culinary Journey
Across India & Beyond

Our in-house restaurant is where flavours of the subcontinent meet. From hearty North Indian dals to crispy South Indian dosas, light Gujarati thalis to sizzling Chinese stir-fries — we celebrate the full range of Indian cuisine under one roof.

Every meal is prepared fresh with locally sourced ingredients where possible. Dine with the Dhauladhar ranges as your backdrop, and let our kitchen turn a meal into a memory.
