About us
A Himalayan retreat amidst apple orchards
Snow City Hotels was founded in 2012 with a simple vision — to offer complete satisfaction and impeccable personalised service in the heart of Himachal Pradesh. Hotel Mountain Face is our flagship property, nestled in apple orchards in Prini, Manali, offering luxury, comfort, and a true escape from the city.
Our founders bring over a decade of hospitality experience and a deep love for the Himalayas to every guest interaction.
