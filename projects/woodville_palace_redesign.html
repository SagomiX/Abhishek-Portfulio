Our Legacy

A Royal Retreat
Since 1938

Perched in the regal heights of Shimla, Woodville Palace has been a witness to history for over eight decades. Built in the colonial era and steeped in aristocratic tradition, the palace has welcomed royalty, dignitaries, and discerning travellers who seek more than just a hotel stay.

Today, as it has always been, Woodville Palace stands as a living monument to Shimla's golden age — where hand-carved woodwork, original heritage furnishings, and breathtaking Himalayan vistas create an experience that cannot be replicated.

"To stay at Woodville Palace is to step into a chapter of history that still breathes, still lives — and still welcomes."

— Heritage Travel India

Our dedicated events team manages every detail — from floral arrangements to royal dining — ensuring your celebration becomes a chapter in the palace's storied history.
