6 key issues — what's broken and how to fix it
1
No room pricing displayed
Every room shows "Price on request." Guests leave immediately when they can't see costs — they go to MakeMyTrip instead.
Fix: Show "from ₹3,500/night" on every room card
2
Outdated visual design
2013-era layout, tiny fonts, no whitespace. Feels untrustworthy for a premium mountain property.
Fix: Full redesign — modern hero, clean cards, new typography
3
Slow page load
Gallery and slider images load at full resolution (3–5 MB each). Site takes 8–12 seconds on mobile networks.
Fix: Compress to WebP, add lazy loading, use a CDN
4
No WhatsApp button
Most Manali travellers prefer quick WhatsApp chats over forms. Missing this loses easy enquiries daily.
Fix: Add a sticky WhatsApp float button sitewide
5
Weak mobile experience
Navigation breaks on phones, text overflows, buttons are too small to tap. 70%+ traffic is mobile.
Fix: Mobile-first responsive layout with thumb-friendly UI
6
No guest reviews on homepage
TripAdvisor CoE badge is hidden in footer. Reviews are the #1 trust signal for hotel bookings.
Fix: Feature 3–4 star reviews prominently on the homepage
