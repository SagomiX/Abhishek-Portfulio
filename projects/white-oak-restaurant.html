About White Oak

Good Food. Great
Vibes. Every Time.

Tucked into Desh Bandhu Gupta Road in Paharganj, White Oak Restaurant is the kind of place Delhi's food lovers keep returning to. Clean interiors, soft music, warm lighting — and food that simply delivers on every visit.

From a full bowl of Kadhai Chicken with buttered naans to a satisfying thali, a cool sweet lassi or a Tootie Frootie Sundae — every meal here feels like a proper occasion. The portions are generous, the service is attentive without being overbearing, and the full bar makes evenings here very easy to extend.
